Oreo Balls

Delicious and chocolate covered!

These are oreo truffles in the shape of a ball.

They taste so good!

Ingredients

  • 1 package cream cheese
  • 36 oreo cookies, finely crushed
  • 16 oz semi-sweet baking chocolate, melted

    • Steps

    1. Mix cream cheese and crushed cookies in a large bowl until well-blended.
    2. Use your hands to shape mixture into 48 (1-inch) balls; place on a tray and freeze until thoroughly chilled, about 10 minutes.
    3. Dip balls in melted chocolate; place in a single layer in a shallow waxed paper-lined pan.
    4. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator.
